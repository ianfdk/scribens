OPERATION

IDENTIFICATION

Facilitating the identification of children with ADHD

The tool makes it possible to compile the observations of the teacher and the parent as soon as there is a doubt about a child regarding any behavioral or attention disorder.

OBJECTIVE MEASUREMENT

Scientific measurement tool for fine motor skills

The technology allows to distinguish neuromuscular control deficits in children with ADHD.

MONITORING

Observing the child\'s evolution over time

It is easy to find out what works and what does not work with medication, adaptive strategies and participants over time through the compilation of subjective and objective data.

COMMUNICATION

All the participants on the same page

The tool allows each stakeholder (eg parent, teacher, resource teacher) to leave notes at the time of each assessment of the child. These notes provide contextual elements to better assess and monitor the child.

SCRIBENS LABORATORY (Polytechnique)

More than 15 years of research on the relationship between human movement and health of the neuromuscular system

The tool was developed in collaboration with the Scribens laboratory of the Polytechnique Montréal. They are currently targeting children who may have ADHD, but could have an impact in the area of ​​neuromuscular health by focusing on people who have suffered concussions or those who suffer from Alzheimer's or Parkinson's.
